Welcome to Faith Tabernacle

An Ark of Salvation for All Nations

Faith Tabernacle is the global headquarters of Living Faith Church Worldwide (Winners' Chapel International), located in Canaanland, Ota, Nigeria. Our mandate is to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching and teaching of the Word of Faith. We invite you to join our vibrant worship services and experience the transforming power of God.

Dynamic Worship

Engage in impactful praise sessions, intense worship and Word encounters in every service.

Prophetic Mandate

We operate under a divine mandate to liberate mankind, unveiling secrets for triumph in life.

Global Impact

With a presence in over 6 continents, the Winners' family is reaching the world with the gospel.
