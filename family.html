Empowering families with kingdom values for a joyful, peaceful and victorious home.

Our Mandate to Families

The Christian Family department of Faith Tabernacle caters to the holistic needs of homes. Through counselling, teachings and interactive sessions, we strengthen marriages, prepare singles and nurture children in the fear of God.

Ministry Expressions

Marriage Counselling

Pre-marital and post-marital counselling sessions anchored by trained ministers.

Singles Fellowship

Building godly relationships, life purpose discovery and destiny partnerships.

Children's Church

Age-graded classes instilling kingdom values, love for God and excellence in children.

Teens Church

Mentoring teenagers for academic success, spiritual growth and leadership development.

Family Resources

  • Family Life teachings during Sunday services and special seminars.
  • Parenting workshops, couples retreats and relationship forums.
  • Books, audio messages and devotionals from Bishop David & Pastor Faith Oyedepo.
