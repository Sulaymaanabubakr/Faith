About WOFBI

The Word of Faith Bible Institute (WOFBI) is a ministry training centre established in 1986 to equip believers with the practical tools needed for exploits in life and ministry. Courses are Holy Ghost inspired, emphasising spiritual depth, character formation and ministry competence.

Schools of Study

Basic Certificate Course (BCC)

Foundational truths of faith, redemption, the Holy Spirit and the believer's authority.

Leadership Certificate Course (LCC)

Training in leadership skills, ministry ethics, soul winning strategies and stewardship.

Leadership Diploma Course (LDC)

Advanced leadership development, ministry structure and organisational management.
