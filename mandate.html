The Mandate

On May 2nd, 1981 the Holy Ghost delivered a mandate to His servant, Bishop David Oyedepo: "The hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith." This divine commission birthed a global movement for the salvation of souls, restoration of dignity and manifestation of signs and wonders.

Four-Fold Impact

Word-Based Ministry

Delivering the unadulterated Word of Faith that builds up believers to enjoy their covenant inheritance.

Prayer & Power

Emphasising fervent prayer, fasting and the ministry of the Holy Spirit for supernatural breakthroughs.

Prosperity & Dominion

Teaching the covenant keys that empower believers for financial dominion and kingdom stewardship.

Healing & Deliverance

Demonstrating the healing power of Jesus that breaks yokes, destroys oppressions and restores health.
