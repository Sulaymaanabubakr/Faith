Our Heritage

Faith Tabernacle, Canaanland is a fulfilment of the heavenly vision delivered to God's servant Bishop David Oyedepo. Founded on May 2nd 1981, Living Faith Church Worldwide continues to impact nations with the message of faith. The 50,000-seat Faith Tabernacle sanctuary, dedicated on September 18th 1999, stands as a testimony of faith in action.

The mandate is to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith. Bishop David Oyedepo

Leadership

Bishop David O. Oyedepo

Founder and President, Living Faith Church Worldwide. Visionary leader and apostle of the Liberation Mandate.

Pastor (Mrs) Faith A. Oyedepo

Vice President (Education) of the ministry and mother of the Winners family globally.

Pastor David O. Oyedepo Jnr.

Resident Pastor, Faith Tabernacle. Leads the vibrant congregation at the international headquarters.

Our Journey

1981

The Liberation Mandate received and Living Faith Church Worldwide founded.

1993

Acquisition of Canaanland, Ota as the new headquarters of the ministry.

1999

Dedication of the 50,000 seat Faith Tabernacle sanctuary to the glory of God.

Present

Global expansion of Winners' Chapel network across 6 continents and 170+ nations.

What to Expect

  • Inspiring worship, prophetic teachings and miracles in every service.
  • A family church with vibrant children, youth, singles and marriage ministries.
  • Ministry expressions for education, humanitarian outreach and missions.
